greggbot updated:

  • Exosuit-mounted hydraulic clamps can now compress scrap into cubes for transport. These cubes can then be whacked with a decent amount of force (a crowbar should do) to decompress them and release the scrap pile. They can also be recycled directly for a reduced amount of materials compared to recycling the contents individually - this amount can be increased with better microlasers, from 25% to 45% with One Star parts.
  • The conveyors disk now has boards for the smelter and sorter. It's also been renamed to logistics, and has license points. Conveyor and switch assemblies still cost 0 LP, so don't worry.
